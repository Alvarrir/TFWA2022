TFWA ASIA PACIFIC EXHIBITION & CONFERENCE 2019

Founded in 1984, TFWA is the world's biggest duty free and travel retail association, providing the industry with high-quality exhibitions, acclaimed conferences and workshops, and in-depth market research.

TFWA Asia Pacific Exhibition & Conference gathers the region’s entire duty free and travel retail industry in one convenient location, providing the ideal environment for networking, sharing knowledge and doing business. It is a must attend event for industry professionals in the region, showcasing hundreds of premium brands and thousands of innovative products at a single, easily accessible venue with world class facilities in the heart of Singapore.
